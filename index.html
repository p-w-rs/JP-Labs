Welcome to Powers Labs

Powers Labs is a forward-thinking research and consulting firm based in Utah, specializing in artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, and interdisciplinary applications. We combine cutting-edge research with practical business solutions to drive innovation.

About Us

Powers Labs is a for-profit LLC registered in Utah, founded by Joshua Powers, who currently serves as the principal researcher. We're in our early stages and growing steadily as we secure more research grants and consulting projects.

Our mission is to pursue innovative and high-value research rather than derivative or incremental work. We believe in creating meaningful advancements that contribute to both academic knowledge and practical applications.

Research Areas

Our research initiatives span multiple disciplines with a focus on emerging technologies:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning - Developing advanced algorithms and models
  • Bio-inspired AI - Learning from natural systems to improve artificial intelligence
  • Embodied Cognition - Exploring the relationship between the mind and physical environment
  • Robotics - Creating intelligent systems for real-world applications
  • Computer Architecture & Operating Systems - Investigating novel computational paradigms
  • Education Systems - Researching improved methods for knowledge transfer and learning

Cross-Disciplinary Research

We actively collaborate with specialists in:

  • Neuroscience
  • Biology
  • Medical Sciences

Our research is primarily funded through federal research grants and programs, allowing us to pursue ambitious goals with long-term scientific value.

Consulting Services

Powers Labs offers specialized consulting to businesses seeking to leverage cutting-edge technology:

  • Machine Vision - Image recognition, object detection, and scene understanding
  • Classification Systems - Data categorization and pattern recognition
  • Recommender Systems - Personalization and preference prediction
  • Robotics Integration - Custom solutions for automation and intelligent systems
  • Natural Language Processing - Text analysis, generation, and understanding
  • Business Process Optimization - AI-driven workflow improvements

We work closely with local companies to develop customized solutions that address specific challenges and create competitive advantages.

Publications
